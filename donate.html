Donate to ReMitts

How to Donate

You can support ReMitts’ efforts to feed hungry people in a variety of ways. Donations of the following are welcome:

  • Wool sweaters – must be 100 percent wool
  • Polar fleece – thin, stretchy type; any color
  • Buttons – all colors, shapes and sizes
  • Thread – all colors
  • Monetary donations – for supplies, tag printing, signs, postage and promotion

Please email remittsmittens@gmail.com to arrange pick up of donations.

Donations to ReMitts are not tax-deductible.
